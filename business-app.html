Business App

Run your coaching business from your pocket.

The fibodo Business App gives coaches, trainers, and instructors everything they need to manage their business on the go. Check your diary, confirm bookings, take payments, and message clients — all from your phone.

Whether you’re at the gym, on the course, in the studio, or travelling between sessions, fibodo keeps you connected to your clients and in control of your day, without needing a laptop.

Built for Life Between Sessions

Your day doesn’t happen behind a desk. The fibodo Business App is designed for coaches who are always on the move — giving you instant access to the tools you need to keep everything running smoothly.

From a quick glance at your diary to managing last-minute changes, you can run your business with a few taps, wherever you are.

What It Does Best

Live Diary in Your Hand

See your daily schedule, upcoming sessions, and client details at a glance — updated in real time from CORE.

On-the-Go Bookings & Edits

Add new bookings, move sessions, or update details in seconds — with everything syncing automatically to your main planner.

Instant Client Management

View client profiles, notes, and history, so you walk into every session prepared and professional.

Mobile Payments

Take payments in person, confirm pre-paid sessions, and track balances — all through the app.

Messaging & Notifications

Send updates, confirmations, and reminders directly to clients, and receive alerts when new bookings or changes come in.
