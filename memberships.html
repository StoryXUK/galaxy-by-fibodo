Memberships that grow your business

Build flexible membership plans

From free trials to premium subscriptions, fibodo lets you create unlimited membership tiers to suit every audience. Set pricing, durations, and sign-up fees, and decide whether your plans are public, private, or invite-only. It’s all designed to scale with your growth.

Reward members with better pricing

Give your members a reason to stay loyal. Apply automatic discounts on bookings, offer exclusive free access to select sessions, or build packages for families and groups. Member pricing is applied instantly at checkout—no promo codes or admin needed.

Gate content and access by membership

Control who sees what. Offer early access to bookings, unlock exclusive on-demand content, or run live sessions just for premium members. From videos to PDFs, everything is protected and personalised based on their membership tier.

Empower members with self-service tools

Members can manage their own plans with ease—from upgrading or cancelling to updating billing details and checking benefits. With smart notifications, automated renewals, and full billing transparency, fibodo makes membership seamless for you and your clients.
