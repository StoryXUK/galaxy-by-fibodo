Key Features

The Everything App offers a powerful blend of free and discounted wellbeing services tailored for NHS and care sector staff. Enjoy on-demand access to fitness, yoga, mindfulness, and resilience content, plus daily live-streamed sessions—all accessible from mobile or desktop. Users can also benefit from at least 25% off in-person classes and memberships. The platform features specialist content for leadership development, stress management, and coaching. It scales to include mental health, social wellbeing, and nutrition support, with dedicated content for families and young people. Staff can even create and join wellbeing groups across NHS organisations.

Strategic Benefits

Designed to strengthen the NHS workforce, The Everything App helps reduce stress, increase satisfaction, and boost staff retention. By offering easily accessible wellbeing support, it promotes physical and mental health in a flexible, inclusive way. The platform’s community features encourage cross-organisation engagement, while its preventative tools can help reduce absenteeism and support long-term resilience.

Customisation & Scalability

The Everything App adapts to meet the unique needs of every organisation. Local ICBs can align it with their workforce priorities, while NHS Trusts can use dedicated dashboards for insight and control. Organisations can choose from a range of service partners—from fitness and nutrition providers to leadership coaches—making the platform fully customisable and scalable for teams of any size.

Performance & Analytics

With built-in analytics, organisations can track wellbeing engagement by staff role, time of day, and content type. Dashboards provide anonymised data to highlight trends, measure impact, and demonstrate ROI. Regular reporting gives People and OD leads the insights needed to evolve strategy, justify investment, and strengthen workforce wellbeing over time.
