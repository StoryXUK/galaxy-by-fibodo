About fibodo

The platform built for the entire experience economy.

fibodo exists to make business simple, for everyone who delivers experiences that make life better. From hairdressers to hot yoga studios, dojos to dog walkers, single-site businesses to global brands, fibodo helps providers spend less time managing and more time doing what they love.
We’re building the operating system for the experience economy, one place to handle bookings, payments, CRM, memberships, content, and communication. Whether you run one location or one hundred, fibodo gives you everything you need to grow, without juggling too many systems or drowning in admin.
Our belief is simple: technology should give time back, not take it away. That’s why fibodo takes care of the complicated parts of business, so you can focus on the people, services, and experiences that matter most.

Why we exist

Across fitness, wellness, and service industries, millions of professionals still juggle spreadsheets, DMs, and payment links just to run their day.

Add in streaming tools, marketing platforms, and accounting software, and suddenly running a business feels like running five.

That fragmentation drains energy, limits income, and stops communities from accessing the expertise that changes lives.

fibodo fixes that, uniting bookings, payments, client management, memberships, content, scheduling, communication, and marketing automation in one intelligent, intuitive hub.

It’s everything you need to run your business, CRM, analytics, your own app and website included, all in one connected platform.

Whether you’re coaching one-to-one, managing a studio, teaching online, or running multiple locations, fibodo helps you save time, grow faster, and deliver better experiences.

Trusted by local businesses & global brands

Proven Impact

fibodo has been building the digital backbone of the experience economy for more than a decade. Born from the energy of the London 2012 Olympics, we set out to make it easier for people to discover and book local activities — and to help professionals turn their skills into sustainable businesses that strengthen communities. An early beta with The Telegraph in 2013 proved the model, followed by powering National Golf Month from 2015 to 2017, helping clubs across the UK attract thousands of new players and boost local participation. By 2018, fibodo was supporting corporate wellbeing programmes for global brands including Sodexo, Diageo, Shell, Jaguar Land Rover, and Johnson & Johnson, expanding our reach from community sport to enterprise wellness. In 2021, we achieved a major milestone — processing over 40,000 bookings each month across fitness, sport, and coaching businesses. And in 2025, our partnership with DoingOurBit (DoB) and NHS Trusts led to the launch of The Everything App, giving healthcare staff and families free access to live and on-demand wellbeing content. Beyond technology, fibodo is driving real-world outcomes — keeping the community pound local, creating job opportunities in health and education, and embedding environmentally responsible innovation that reduces waste, travel, and operational impact. Each milestone reinforces our mission: when you make it easier for people to deliver what they love, everyone benefits — economically, socially, and sustainably.
