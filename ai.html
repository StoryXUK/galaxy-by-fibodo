Overview

AI in fibodo works quietly in the background, learning from your bookings, memberships, and revenue patterns. It helps you fill quieter times, protect peak hours, reduce churn, and communicate with members in a more personal way – at scale.

  • Spot demand patterns and optimise schedules with smarter suggestions
  • Adjust pricing, offers, and bundles based on real behaviour, not guesswork
  • Predict who’s at risk of leaving and trigger the right save campaigns
  • Generate on-brand content and campaigns faster, from social posts to emails

You stay in control of the rules. AI surfaces recommendations, automates repeat tasks, and explains what’s happening – so decisions are still human, just better informed.

Key Functionality

Smart Scheduling

Use historic bookings and trends to suggest optimal times for classes, events, and 1:1s, helping you avoid empty slots and protect high-demand sessions.

Dynamic Pricing & Yield

Set rules that nudge prices up during peak demand and unlock offers when things are quiet, so each hour works harder without constant manual tweaks.

Recommendations & Bundles

Recommend relevant classes, courses, and add-ons at checkout or in follow-up campaigns based on what similar customers have booked and enjoyed.

Churn & Retention Signals

Identify members who are slipping (missed sessions, reduced visits, cancelled renewals) and trigger save journeys with tailored offers, content, or check-ins.

AI-Powered Messaging

Draft emails, push notifications, and campaign ideas from simple prompts. Tweak the tone, length, and audience before sending via your existing comms tools.

Segmentation & Targeting

Automatically group customers by behaviour, value, goals, and engagement, then target each segment with more relevant content, offers, and programmes.

Forecasting & Planning

See projected revenue, attendance, and membership trends, helping you plan staffing, new locations, or product launches with more confidence.

Anomaly & Fraud Signals

Flag unusual booking, refund, or voucher activity that might indicate misuse or errors, so you can act quickly before it turns into a bigger issue.

Natural Language Insights

Ask questions in plain English (“Which membership type brings the highest lifetime value?”) and get clear answers based on your fibodo data.

Content & Product Suggestions

Get ideas for new classes, challenges, and programmes based on what’s popular, what’s growing, and where you have gaps in the schedule.

AI Across the Stack

AI connects to Planner, Product Library, CRM, Payments, and Rewards, so the same intelligence powers your bookings, pricing, loyalty, and communications.
