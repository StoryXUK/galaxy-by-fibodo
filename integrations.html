Overview

Your data shouldn’t live in silos. With fibodo Integrations, bookings and memberships become the heartbeat of your wider ecosystem — from CRM and accounting to marketing tools, wearables, and access control.

  • Sync customer and booking data into CRM to power sales and retention journeys
  • Post revenue, refunds, and tax summaries into accounting software automatically
  • Connect to email and marketing platforms for smarter, behaviour-led campaigns
  • Link wearables and access systems so visits, check-ins, and activity are captured in one place

No more exporting spreadsheets, chasing duplicates, or re-keying data. fibodo becomes the operational core, while your other systems do what they do best.

Key Functionality

CRM Integrations

Keep contact records, memberships, and activity timelines in sync with your CRM so sales, customer service, and marketing teams can see the full picture.

Accounting & Finance

Send takings, payouts, fees, and tax lines straight into accounting platforms to reduce manual reconciliation and speed up month-end.

Email & Marketing Platforms

Push segments and events (new member, lapsed, high-value, challenge joined) into your email and automation tools for timely, relevant campaigns.

Wearables & Activity Tracking

Connect with wearables and fitness tracking solutions so you can link real-world activity, streaks, and goals back to memberships and programmes.

Access Control & Check-In

Integrate with access control or gate systems so active members and pass holders can tap in, with usage pushed back into fibodo for reporting and rewards.

Payment & Payout Feeds

Align payment gateways and payout data with your finance and BI tools so revenue, fees, and liabilities stay consistent across platforms.

HR & Payroll Links

Share timesheets, sessions delivered, and commission information with HR and payroll systems where applicable, to reduce admin and disputes.

Data Warehouse & BI

Feed fibodo data into your BI, data warehouse, or reporting stack so you can combine it with other business metrics for deeper insight.

APIs & Webhooks

Use APIs and event-based webhooks to build custom workflows and integrations for your specific needs or partners.

Configuration & Mapping

Control which fields sync, how products map, and how often data moves, so each integration behaves the way your business needs it to.

Monitoring & Error Handling

Track integration health, see when syncs run, and get alerted if something needs attention, so you can trust the data you see elsewhere.
